陶之始之一 Clay Origin I

樹精初生 The First Birth of Tree Spirits

早期陶土先以樹精的姿態甦醒，從昂首、懷抱與生長的身體中，慢慢建立又禎宇宙最初的生命語彙。 Early clay first awakens as tree spirits, gradually forming the first life vocabulary of the Yuzen Universe through lifted heads, embraces, and growing bodies.

陶之始之二 Clay Origin II

容器與內在空間 Vessels and Inner Space

孔洞、臉孔與內部空間開始交疊，陶土不再只是外形，而成為承接生命、情緒與人格雛形的容器。 Openings, faces, and inner space begin to overlap; clay is no longer only outward form, but a vessel receiving life, emotion, and the first shapes of persona.

作為早期陶作之一，《虛實之間》已出現後來「人格考古學」中臉孔、孔洞與內在空間交錯的原型。 As an early clay work, Between Virtual and Real already contains the prototype of faces, openings, and inner spaces that later appear in Archaeology of Persona.

陶之始保留又禎宇宙最早以陶土靠近生命的痕跡：樹精、孔洞、臉孔與容器都還帶著初生的單純。 Clay Origin preserves the earliest traces of the Yuzen Universe approaching life through clay: tree spirits, openings, faces, and vessels still carry the simplicity of first birth.

這些作品像後來「陶與生命容器」與「人格考古學」的前奏，讓生命形象第一次從手的重量中浮現。 These works are preludes to Clay and Vessels of Life and Archaeology of Persona, allowing life images to first emerge through the weight of the hand.
