生命的顯現 Manifestation of Life

生命的顯現，是我在世界留下的痕跡中，看見生命逐漸浮現的系列。石頭、樹木、舊牆、海岸與時間的紋理，不只是風景，而是生命正在變形、聚集與回望的場所。 Manifestation of Life is a series in which I see life gradually emerging from the traces left by the world. Stones, trees, old walls, coastlines, and the textures of time are not only landscapes, but places where life transforms, gathers, and looks back.

系列收束 Series Closing

生命在痕跡中回望 Life Looking Back Through Traces

從石板、牆面、身體、海岸到樹木與布料，這個系列把世界表面的裂縫看成生命浮現的入口。 From stone slab, wall, body, and coast to tree and fabric, this series sees cracks on the world's surfaces as entrances where life appears.

每一次觀看都不是把物件變成形象，而是在已經存在的痕跡裡，辨認那些安靜等待被看見的生命。 Each act of seeing does not turn an object into an image; it recognizes lives already waiting quietly within existing traces.
