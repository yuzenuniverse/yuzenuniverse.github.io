插畫立體化 Illustration to Sculpture

由平面插畫轉化為立體陶塑。 From flat illustration into ceramic sculpture.

將收錄

由平面插畫轉化為立體陶塑，之後可依代表作品逐步放入。Illustrations transformed into ceramic sculpture can be added here.

整理方向

保留插畫如何從平面線條成為可被觀看、繞行的陶塑形體。Tracing how drawings become ceramic forms that can be viewed in the round.
