人格地層 Persona Layer

陶塑人格層 Ceramic Persona Layer

從線稿轉成陶塑、立體角色與可被繞行的人格，使公仔也成為生命容器。 Ceramic figures and walk-around personas as vessels of character and life.

將收錄

陶作公仔、小型立體角色與從線稿轉化而來的人格容器。Small ceramic figures, sculptural roles, and persona vessels transformed from drawings.

整理方向

讓每件陶塑不只是物件，而是能被靠近、繞行與辨認的角色。Framing each ceramic figure as a role that can be approached and recognized.
