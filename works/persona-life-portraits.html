生命群像層讓人格不再孤立，每一件作品都像一個小角色，帶著自己的表情、位置與等待。 The Life Portraits Layer keeps persona from isolation. Each work becomes a small figure with its own expression, position, and waiting.

當它們被並置觀看，整個系列像一場安靜的聚會，讓不同生命在同一個展場中彼此照亮。 When viewed together, the series becomes a quiet gathering in which different lives illuminate one another.
