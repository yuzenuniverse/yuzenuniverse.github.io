古文明的裂生 Ancient Civilization Reborn Through Cracks

古文明的裂生，是我在殘片、裂縫與時間的沉積中，看見文明重新生成的系列。它們不是被復原的遺跡，而是從破碎之處長出的生命形態。 Ancient Civilization Reborn Through Cracks is a series in which I see civilization regenerating within the sediments of fragments, cracks, and time. They are not restored ruins, but forms of life growing out of broken places.
