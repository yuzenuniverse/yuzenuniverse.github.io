古文明的裂生 Ancient Civilization Reborn Through Cracks

古文明的裂生，是我在殘片、裂縫與時間的沉積中，看見文明重新生成的系列。它們不是被復原的遺跡，而是從破碎之處長出的生命形態。 Ancient Civilization Reborn Through Cracks is a series in which I see civilization regenerating within the sediments of fragments, cracks, and time. They are not restored ruins, but forms of life growing out of broken places.

策展入口 Curated Entry

從裂縫中重新誕生的文明 A Civilization Reborn from Cracks

這個系列的觀看可以從裂縫開始，沿著神話、殘片、柱體與複合身體逐步深入。陶土在這裡不是重建遺跡，而是讓失落文明以新的生命形態再次顯現。 The viewing begins with cracks, then moves through myth, fragments, pillars, and composite bodies. Here, clay does not rebuild ruins; it allows lost civilization to reappear as a new form of life.

裂縫不是破壞的終點，而是文明重新顯影的開口；神話、海洋與傾聽在這裡成為第一層入口。 A crack is not the end of destruction, but an opening where civilization appears again. Myth, ocean, and listening become the first threshold.
